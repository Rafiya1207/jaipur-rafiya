# Jaipur-Market
***Trade your skills to be Best trader***

> **_Jaipur..._** You are hoping to become the Maharaja’s personal trader by being richer than your opponent at the end of each week (round).  
> To do so, collect and exchange goods (cards) at the market, then sell them for rupees (on the back of the tokens).  
> If you manage to puttogether a really good deal (three cards or more), then you’ll receive
a reward (bonus token).  
> As for the camels, they are mainly used for exchanges when you want to take multiple goods at the market.  
> At the end of each round, the richer trader receives a Seal of Excellence .  
> **The first player to collect 2 of these wins the game.**
